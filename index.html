With over a decade of intensive study in traditional Islamic sciences, I have dedicated my life to understanding and teaching the profound wisdom of Islam. My journey began at a young age in a traditional madrasa, where I immersed myself in the study of the Quran, Hadith, and classical Islamic texts.

I hold multiple Ijazahs (certifications) in Quranic recitation, Hadith transmission, and various Islamic sciences. My passion lies in making Islamic knowledge accessible to modern audiences while maintaining the authenticity and depth of traditional scholarship.
